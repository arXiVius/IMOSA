IMOSA (Indonesia Mountain Specialist)
Overview
IMOSA is an Indonesia-based company specializing in mountain expeditions and adventure travel.
They organize and guide climbs across Indonesia, ranging from trekking peaks to highly technical expeditions.

The company operates as a professional expedition organizer, delivering structured climbing experiences with a focus on preparation, execution, and safety.

---

Founding & Background
IMOSA was established in 2013 by a group of Indonesian climbers.

The founders completed the Seven Summits (the highest peaks on each continent) between 2010 and 2012.
This experience shaped the company’s approach by introducing international expedition standards into Indonesia.

The company was created to:
Improve the quality of mountain guiding in Indonesia
Provide structured expedition support
Enable access to remote and challenging mountains

Expedition Structure
Preparation Phase
Trip details are provided
Requirements are defined (fitness, gear, experience)
Logistics are arranged

Climbing Phase
Guides lead the expedition
Support teams assist operations
Camps are established along the route
Movement follows a structured itinerary

Completion Phase
Descent is managed
Return logistics are handled
Expedition concludes at the exit point
