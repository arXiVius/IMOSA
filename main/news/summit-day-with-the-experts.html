Come and curl with the Gods on Sunday 10th!

Join the IMOSA. for the world-famous in Indonesia (and beyond) IMOSA Mountain Challenge Day on Sunday 10th August 2025.

Held on the Tarn, 1800 metres above sea level at the top of the expedition site!

Enter a team on the day or join one when you arrive! Fancy dress is optional (and encouraged). Let your friends and family know.

All ages welcome!

What you need to know:

  • Register your team by 11am. If you don't have a team put your name down and we will find you one. No experience is needed!
  • Heli lifts leaving from Bottom Hut from 10am. Email info@imosa.id to book ($160 pp – Heli, or $180 pp for Heli & lunch. Pay on the day).
  • We will have a porta-toilet at the tarn for the day.
  • Pre-order (via email info@imosa.id) a picnic lunch of hot soup and a sandwich – these will be delivered to the tarn on the day.
  • Full lunch is still available in the Top Hut, again, order by 11am.
  • 'Cold Soup' (AKA Refreshments) will be available at the Tarn, bring cash.

It will be a bonnie day in the Summit of Adventure! See you there!
